section 4 of the probation of offenders act1958 power of court to release certain offenders on probation of good conduct. section 4 of the pob act applies to all kinds of offenders whether under or above 21 years of age. this section 4 of the pob act is intended to attempt possible reformation of an offender instead of inflicting on him the normal punishment of his crime. the only limitation imposed by section 6 of the pob act is that in the first instance an offender under twenty one years of age will not be sentenced to imprisonment.
